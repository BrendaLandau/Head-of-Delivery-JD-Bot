Job Description
Head of Delivery Operations
Leadership Team  ·  Reports to: CEO
About CI

CI is an AI-first technology company that designs, builds, and operates AI-powered digital systems for enterprise and mid-market organizations in regulated industries.

Position summary

This role owns delivery vision, strategy, and implementation across CI. It is a Leadership Team seat with full accountability for how CI delivers, at what quality, and at what velocity. The right candidate brings operational rigor, business and financial literacy, and enough technical grounding to lead AI-first delivery credibly. This is not a role for a single-dimension operator.

Delivery vision, strategy and implementation
  • Define and own CI's delivery vision and translate it into a clear executable strategy
  • Build the delivery roadmap across all delivery models with quarterly milestones
  • Lead delivery implementation end to end from standards-setting through measurement
  • Own the delivery operating model and drive it to completion
  • Ensure delivery strategy is aligned to sales, financial goals, technical standards, and AI adoption objectives
Core responsibilities
  • Own and lead the delivery organization, driving team performance, accountability, and execution standards across all delivery functions
  • Drive delivery velocity, margin performance, and client satisfaction
  • Lead AI-first delivery execution including adoption targets and delivery standards
  • Establish delivery governance structures and execution frameworks that scale
  • Coordinate cross-functional execution across sales, engineering, AI and Development, and clients
  • Partner closely with the leadership team to execute strategic operational initiatives
What you will step into

Build first, run second. Come in with a vision, build the strategy, and then own the delivery organization you designed. If you are looking for a traditional PMO role, this is not it. If you want to own delivery end to end and build the function around you, it is.

What we are looking for
  • 7+ years of delivery or operations leadership in technology services, SaaS, or AI-driven environments, with at least 3 years in a senior seat and 6 to 12 months of demonstrable hands-on AI tool experience
  • Operational delivery leadership experience inside enterprise technology, SaaS, AI-driven organizations, or digital transformation consulting
  • Demonstrated experience setting delivery vision and strategy, not just managing delivery execution
  • Experience working directly with a Leadership Team and contributing as a peer, not a layer below
  • Ability to build operational frameworks from the ground up in a fast-moving environment
  • Strong cross-functional leadership across delivery, sales, engineering, and client-facing teams
  • Executive communication and client-facing presence
  • Familiarity with structured operating frameworks such as EOS or Scaling Up
  • Proficiency with Salesforce and a PSA platform is required. Certinia (formerly FinancialForce) experience is strongly preferred
  • Active use of AI productivity tools is expected. Candidates should be current AI tool users, not AI-curious observers
  • Proven people leadership experience. You will build and lead the delivery team you design
  • Sufficient platform fluency to lead delivery credibly across CI's technology stack, including Salesforce and Certinia. You do not need to be an engineer, but you need to understand how these platforms work well enough to build delivery procedures
